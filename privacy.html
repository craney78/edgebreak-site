    Our Commitment to Your Privacy

    Protecting your personal information is a fundamental part of the EdgeBreak platform. Whether you're researching NASDAQ stocks, managing your workspace or connecting a supported brokerage account, we are committed to handling your information responsibly, transparently and securely.

    This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, how it is protected and the choices available to you when using EdgeBreak. We aim to provide clear and transparent information so you always understand how your data is handled.

    EdgeBreak is operated by Crane Platform Labs and provides stock research tools, portfolio management features, educational resources and secure broker connectivity through supported third-party brokerage providers using OAuth authentication.

    Privacy First

    EdgeBreak never requests or stores your brokerage username or password. Broker authentication is performed securely through OAuth provided by supported brokerage partners, ensuring your login credentials remain with your broker.

    Information We Collect

    Depending on how you use EdgeBreak, we may collect information necessary to provide our research platform, manage your account, improve our services and support secure platform functionality.

    • Account Information including your name, email address and account credentials when you register for an EdgeBreak account.
    • Subscription Information including subscription status and payment records. Payment information is processed securely by Stripe and is not stored by EdgeBreak.
    • Platform Activity including pages visited, watchlists, saved research, journal entries, workspace preferences and other platform features you choose to use.
    • Technical Information including browser type, operating system, device information, IP address, session information and diagnostic data used to maintain platform performance and security.
    • Support Communications including emails, feedback, feature requests and other information you voluntarily provide when contacting EdgeBreak.
    • Broker Connection Information when you choose to connect a supported brokerage account. This may include account identifiers, portfolio information, positions, balances, orders and transaction history necessary to provide authorised platform features. EdgeBreak never collects or stores your brokerage username or password.

    How We Use Your Information

    EdgeBreak uses your information only for legitimate business purposes related to operating the platform, delivering services you request and maintaining a secure user experience.

    • Provide Platform Features including access to research tools, watchlists, portfolio management, journal features and other EdgeBreak services.
    • Manage Your Account including authentication, subscriptions, billing records and customer support.
    • Maintain Platform Security by detecting suspicious activity, preventing unauthorised access and protecting user accounts.
    • Support Broker Connectivity by securely synchronising authorised brokerage information required to provide connected portfolio and trading features.
    • Improve EdgeBreak by understanding how platform features are used, identifying technical issues and enhancing the overall user experience.
    • Communicate With You regarding important account notifications, security alerts, platform updates and customer support enquiries.

    Our Commitment

    EdgeBreak does not sell your personal information to third parties. Your information is used only to operate, improve and secure the EdgeBreak platform and to provide the services you choose to use.

    Broker Connections & OAuth Authentication

    EdgeBreak may allow you to securely connect supported third-party brokerage accounts to provide portfolio management, order management and other authorised platform features. Broker connections are entirely optional and are initiated only with your explicit consent.

    • Secure OAuth Authentication – Broker authentication is completed directly with your selected brokerage provider using OAuth. EdgeBreak never requests, receives or stores your brokerage username or password.
    • Authorised Access – EdgeBreak only accesses the information and permissions you authorise through your broker.
    • Broker Information – Where authorised, EdgeBreak may access account balances, portfolio holdings, positions, orders, transaction history and related account information required to provide platform features.
    • Disconnecting Your Broker – You may disconnect a supported brokerage account at any time. Disconnecting a broker will stop future synchronisation, although certain records may be retained where required for security, operational or legal purposes.

    Your Broker Credentials Stay With Your Broker

    EdgeBreak never has access to your brokerage username or password. Authentication is performed securely by your selected broker using OAuth technology.

    Payments

    EdgeBreak uses Stripe to securely process subscription payments. Stripe is an industry-leading payment processor that manages payment transactions using secure encryption and PCI-compliant infrastructure.

    • Secure Payment Processing – All subscription payments are processed directly by Stripe using encrypted payment technology.
    • No Card Storage – EdgeBreak does not collect, store or have access to your complete credit card or debit card details.
    • Subscription Management – We retain only the information necessary to manage your subscription, payment status and account access.
    • Billing Records – Basic transaction information may be retained for accounting, customer support and legal compliance purposes.

    Payment Security

    Your payment information is transmitted securely to Stripe and is never stored on EdgeBreak's servers. This helps protect your financial information while providing a secure subscription experience.

    Cookies & Similar Technologies

    EdgeBreak uses cookies and similar technologies to provide essential platform functionality, improve your experience and help maintain the security and performance of the platform.

    • Essential Cookies – Required for core website functionality, secure authentication and maintaining your session while using EdgeBreak.
    • Preference Cookies – Used to remember settings and personalise your experience where applicable.
    • Analytics – Anonymous usage information may be collected to help us understand how visitors use the platform and identify opportunities for improvement.
    • Security – Certain technologies may be used to help detect suspicious activity, protect user accounts and maintain platform security.

    Managing Cookies

    Most web browsers allow you to manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the availability or functionality of certain EdgeBreak features.

    Data Security

    Protecting your personal information is a core priority at EdgeBreak. We use industry standard security practices and continually review our systems to help protect your information, maintain platform integrity and reduce the risk of unauthorised access.

    • Secure Connections – Data transmitted between your device and EdgeBreak is encrypted using HTTPS and secure communication protocols.
    • Secure Authentication – User authentication is managed through secure authentication services designed to help protect your account and personal information.
    • Broker Authentication – Supported brokerage accounts connect securely using OAuth. EdgeBreak never requests or stores your brokerage username or password.
    • Access Controls – Access to personal information is restricted to authorised processes and services required to operate the EdgeBreak platform.
    • Ongoing Security Improvements – We regularly review and improve our security practices as new platform features and technologies are introduced.

    Security Commitment

    While we work continuously to maintain a secure platform, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. We encourage users to choose strong passwords, keep their login details confidential and promptly report any suspected unauthorised account activity.

    International Data Transfers

    EdgeBreak uses trusted technology providers to deliver secure, reliable and high-performance services. As a result, some personal information may be processed or stored in countries outside Australia, including the United States and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate.

    • Trusted Service Providers – We work only with reputable third-party providers that support essential platform services such as authentication, payment processing, cloud infrastructure and broker connectivity.
    • Security Standards – Where information is transferred internationally, we take reasonable steps to ensure it is handled securely and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
    • Platform Services – International processing may occur when using services such as secure account authentication, subscription management, cloud hosting or authorised brokerage integrations.

    Our Commitment

    Regardless of where your information is processed, EdgeBreak is committed to protecting your personal information and working with service providers that maintain appropriate security and privacy standards.

    Data Retention

    EdgeBreak retains personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain platform security and enforce our agreements.

    • Account Information – Retained while your EdgeBreak account remains active.
    • Subscription Records – May be retained for accounting, taxation and regulatory purposes.
    • Broker Connection Information – Information associated with connected brokerage accounts may be retained only as required to provide authorised platform features or meet legal and operational obligations.
    • Account Closure – When your account is closed, we will securely delete or anonymise personal information where appropriate, unless we are required to retain certain records by law.

    Responsible Data Retention

    We retain personal information only for as long as it is reasonably necessary to operate the EdgeBreak platform, comply with applicable legal obligations and protect the security and integrity of our services.

    Your Privacy Rights

    Depending on your location and the privacy laws that apply to you, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. EdgeBreak is committed to responding to privacy requests in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

    • Access Your Information – Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
    • Correct Your Information – Request that inaccurate or incomplete personal information be updated or corrected.
    • Delete Your Information – Request deletion of your personal information where permitted by applicable laws and our legal obligations.
    • Manage Communications – Withdraw consent for marketing communications or update your communication preferences at any time.
    • Close Your Account – Request the closure of your EdgeBreak account, subject to any legal, regulatory or operational record-keeping requirements.

    Need Assistance?

    If you would like to exercise your privacy rights or have questions about how your personal information is handled, please contact us and we will respond as soon as reasonably practicable.

    Third-Party Services

    EdgeBreak works with carefully selected third-party service providers to deliver secure, reliable and efficient platform functionality. These providers perform services on our behalf and may process information only as required to deliver the services you choose to use.

    • Authentication Services – Secure account authentication and user management.
    • Payment Processing – Subscription billing and payment processing through trusted payment providers.
    • Cloud Infrastructure – Secure hosting, database management and platform infrastructure.
    • Broker Connectivity – Secure OAuth connections with supported brokerage partners for authorised portfolio, account and trading features.
    • Analytics & Performance – Anonymous usage information may be used to improve platform performance, reliability and user experience.

    Trusted Providers

    Our third-party providers maintain their own privacy and security practices. Where appropriate, we work with providers that support recognised security standards and are committed to protecting customer information.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    As EdgeBreak continues to evolve, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our platform, services, supported broker integrations, security practices or legal and regulatory obligations.

    When material changes are made, we will update the Last Updated date shown on this page. Where appropriate, we may also notify registered users through the EdgeBreak platform or by email.

    Staying Informed

    We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically so you remain informed about how EdgeBreak protects your personal information and any changes to our privacy practices.
