    Our Commitment to Your Privacy

    EdgeBreak is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, how it is protected and the choices available to you when using the EdgeBreak platform.

    EdgeBreak is operated by Crane Platform Labs and is designed as a NASDAQ stock research platform providing educational resources and structured market research tools.

    Information We Collect

    Depending on how you use EdgeBreak, we may collect information including:

    • Email address and account details when creating an account.
    • Subscription and billing information processed securely through Stripe.
    • Platform usage information including pages viewed and features accessed.
    • Technical information such as browser type, device type and IP address.
    • Information you provide when contacting our support team.

    How We Use Your Information

    Your information helps us:

    • Provide access to EdgeBreak features and services.
    • Maintain your account and subscription.
    • Improve platform performance and user experience.
    • Respond to enquiries and support requests.
    • Maintain platform security and prevent misuse.
    • Communicate important service updates.

    Payments

    Payments are processed securely through Stripe. EdgeBreak does not store or have access to your complete credit card or payment information.

    Cookies and Analytics

    EdgeBreak may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, remember preferences and better understand how visitors use the platform.

    You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

    Data Security

    We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration or loss.

    While we work to maintain a secure platform, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

    International Data Transfers

    EdgeBreak uses trusted third-party infrastructure providers that may process or store information in multiple countries, including Australia and the United States.

    Your Privacy Rights

    Subject to applicable laws, you may have the right to:

    • Request access to your personal information.
    • Request correction of inaccurate information.
    • Request deletion of personal information where legally permitted.
    • Withdraw consent for certain communications.

    Third-Party Services

    EdgeBreak relies on trusted third-party providers including Supabase, Stripe and analytics services to deliver parts of the platform. These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

    Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our platform, services or legal obligations. Updates become effective once published on this page.

    Questions About Privacy?

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us.

    admin@edgebreak.ai

    We aim to respond to privacy enquiries as promptly as possible.
