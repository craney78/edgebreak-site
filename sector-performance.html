Why Sector Performance Matters

Strong stocks are often found within strong sectors. Monitoring sector performance can help identify areas of the market attracting buying interest before individual breakout opportunities become widely recognised.

Sector strength should be considered alongside trend analysis, price structure, volume and overall market conditions. No single indicator should be used in isolation when researching investment opportunities.

EdgeBreak uses sector analysis as one component of a broader research process designed to help organise and prioritise potential opportunities.
