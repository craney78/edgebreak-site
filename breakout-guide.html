Introduction

Breakout trading focuses on identifying when price moves from consolidation into expansion.

Market Structure and Build-Up

  • Multiple resistance tests
  • Higher lows forming
  • Tightening price range

Volume Confirmation

  • Low volume = consolidation
  • High volume = breakout confirmation

Execution

  • Enter on breakout close
  • Or next candle continuation

False Breakouts

  • Weak breakout candles
  • Immediate rejection
  • Falls below resistance
