    Our Security Commitment

    Protecting your account, personal information and connected services is a core priority at EdgeBreak. We design our platform with security in mind and continually review our systems, infrastructure and operational practices to help protect user information and maintain the integrity of our services.

    EdgeBreak uses trusted technologies and industry-standard security practices to safeguard authentication, encrypted communications, data storage and broker connectivity. As the platform continues to evolve, we remain committed to strengthening our security controls and responding to emerging threats.

    While no online platform can guarantee absolute security, we work continuously to minimise risk through secure system design, regular monitoring and responsible security practices.

    Security First

    Security is integrated throughout the EdgeBreak platform—from secure account authentication and encrypted communications to protected broker connections and continuous platform monitoring. Our goal is to provide a trusted environment where users can research financial markets with confidence.

    Account Security

    Protecting your EdgeBreak account starts with secure authentication and responsible account management. While we implement security measures designed to protect user accounts, you also play an important role in maintaining the security of your account and personal information.

    • Secure Authentication – EdgeBreak uses secure authentication technologies to help protect user accounts and verify authorised access.
    • Strong Passwords – We recommend choosing a unique, complex password that is not used for any other online account.
    • Confidential Credentials – Never share your EdgeBreak login credentials with anyone. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account information.
    • Session Security – Authentication sessions are managed securely to help reduce the risk of unauthorised access while using the platform.
    • Suspicious Activity – If you believe your account has been compromised or accessed without your permission, please change your password immediately and contact our support team.
    • Future Security Enhancements – As EdgeBreak evolves, additional account protection features such as multi-factor authentication and enhanced login security may be introduced to further strengthen account protection.

    Protect Your Account

    Choosing a strong password, keeping your login credentials confidential and reporting suspicious activity promptly are some of the most effective ways to help keep your EdgeBreak account secure.

    Authentication & Access Control

    EdgeBreak uses secure authentication and access control mechanisms to help ensure that only authorised users can access their accounts and platform features. Access permissions are managed carefully to protect user information while maintaining a secure and reliable platform experience.

    • Secure Login – User authentication is managed using secure authentication technologies designed to verify authorised access and protect user accounts.
    • Session Management – Secure sessions help protect your account while you are signed in and reduce the risk of unauthorised access.
    • Access Permissions – Platform features and account information are accessible only after successful authentication and are limited to authorised users.
    • Least-Privilege Access – Internal access to systems and user information is restricted to authorised processes and personnel who require access to operate, maintain or secure the EdgeBreak platform.
    • Continuous Improvements – We regularly review our authentication and access control practices and may introduce additional security measures, including multi-factor authentication, enhanced login verification and other account protection features as the platform evolves.
    • User Responsibility – You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and ensuring that your account is accessed only by you.

    Secure Access

    Authentication and access controls help ensure that only authorised users can access EdgeBreak accounts and platform features, providing an additional layer of protection for your information and connected services.

    Broker Security & OAuth Authentication

    EdgeBreak is designed to integrate securely with supported third-party brokerage providers using industry-standard OAuth authentication. This approach allows you to authorise access to your brokerage account without sharing your brokerage login credentials with EdgeBreak.

    • OAuth Authentication – When connecting a supported brokerage account, authentication takes place directly with your selected broker using secure OAuth technology.
    • No Broker Passwords – EdgeBreak never requests, receives or stores your brokerage username, password or other confidential login credentials.
    • Authorised Access Only – EdgeBreak can access only the information and permissions that you explicitly authorise through your brokerage provider.
    • Secure Data Synchronisation – Portfolio information, account balances, positions, orders and other authorised account information are transmitted securely between your broker and the EdgeBreak platform.
    • Disconnect At Any Time – You may revoke EdgeBreak's access to a connected brokerage account at any time through the Broker Centre or by disconnecting the integration with your brokerage provider.
    • Independent Broker Security – Brokerage providers maintain their own security infrastructure, authentication systems and regulatory obligations. You remain subject to your broker's own security policies and terms of service.

    Your Broker Credentials Stay With Your Broker

    EdgeBreak never stores or has access to your brokerage username or password. Secure OAuth authentication ensures your login credentials remain with your brokerage provider while allowing authorised platform features to operate safely.

    Data Protection & Encryption

    Protecting your information is a fundamental part of the EdgeBreak platform. We use secure technologies, encrypted communications and trusted infrastructure providers to help safeguard personal information, account data and authorised broker connectivity throughout the platform.

    • Encrypted Connections – Information transmitted between your device and the EdgeBreak platform is protected using HTTPS and industry-standard Transport Layer Security (TLS) encryption.
    • Secure Infrastructure – EdgeBreak is hosted using trusted cloud infrastructure providers that implement recognised security controls to help protect platform availability, reliability and stored information.
    • Protected Data Storage – Personal information, account data and authorised platform information are stored using secure systems designed to help prevent unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration or loss.
    • Restricted Access – Access to sensitive information is limited to authorised systems and personnel who require access to operate, maintain and secure the EdgeBreak platform.
    • Secure Broker Communications – Where broker connectivity is enabled, authorised information is transmitted securely between EdgeBreak and supported brokerage providers using encrypted communication channels.
    • Continuous Security Improvements – We regularly review our security architecture and adopt appropriate technologies and practices to strengthen the protection of user information as the platform evolves.

    Protecting Your Information

    From encrypted communications and secure cloud infrastructure to controlled system access and protected broker connections, EdgeBreak is designed with multiple layers of security to help safeguard your information and maintain the integrity of the platform.

    Platform Monitoring & Incident Response

    EdgeBreak continuously monitors the platform to help maintain security, reliability and availability. While no online service can eliminate every risk, we aim to identify potential security issues promptly, respond appropriately and continually strengthen our security practices as the platform evolves.

    • Continuous Monitoring – Platform activity and system performance are monitored to help identify potential security threats, technical issues and unusual account activity.
    • Security Reviews – We periodically review our systems, infrastructure and security controls to identify opportunities for improvement and strengthen the protection of user information.
    • Incident Response – Where a security incident is identified, we investigate the issue, take appropriate action to contain and resolve it, and implement measures designed to reduce the likelihood of similar incidents occurring in the future.
    • Platform Updates – Security improvements, software updates and infrastructure enhancements are regularly deployed to improve platform stability, performance and protection.
    • User Notifications – Where appropriate and required by applicable law, affected users may be notified of significant security incidents or events that could impact their EdgeBreak account or personal information.
    • Continuous Improvement – Security is an ongoing process. As technology, cyber threats and industry standards evolve, EdgeBreak will continue to review and improve its security practices.

    Security Never Stops

    Protecting the EdgeBreak platform is an ongoing commitment. We continually monitor, review and improve our systems to help provide a secure, reliable and trustworthy environment for every user.

    Your Role in Security

    Security is a shared responsibility. While EdgeBreak implements security measures designed to protect the platform and your information, the actions you take also play an important role in keeping your account secure and reducing the risk of unauthorised access.

    • Choose a Strong Password – Create a unique, complex password for your EdgeBreak account and avoid reusing passwords from other online services.
    • Keep Your Credentials Private – Never share your login credentials with anyone and do not allow others to access your EdgeBreak account.
    • Protect Your Devices – Keep your computer, mobile device and web browser up to date with the latest security updates and use appropriate antivirus or security software where applicable.
    • Be Alert to Phishing – Be cautious of emails, messages or websites requesting your login details or personal information. EdgeBreak will never ask you to disclose your password by email.
    • Monitor Your Account – Regularly review your account activity and connected broker integrations. If you notice anything unusual, change your password immediately and contact us.
    • Report Security Concerns – If you believe your account has been compromised or you identify a potential security issue affecting EdgeBreak, please notify us as soon as reasonably practicable.

    Security Starts With You

    Simple actions such as using a strong password, protecting your devices, recognising phishing attempts and reporting suspicious activity can significantly improve the security of your EdgeBreak account and help protect the wider platform community.

    Reporting Security Concerns

    We take security seriously and encourage responsible reporting of suspected security vulnerabilities, unauthorised account activity or other security concerns. Prompt reporting helps us investigate issues quickly and maintain the security and integrity of the EdgeBreak platform.

    • Report Suspicious Activity – If you believe your EdgeBreak account has been accessed without your permission or you notice unusual account activity, please contact us immediately.
    • Report Security Vulnerabilities – If you discover a potential security vulnerability affecting the EdgeBreak platform, we encourage you to report it responsibly so it can be investigated and addressed.
    • Responsible Disclosure – We ask that security researchers and users report potential vulnerabilities privately and allow reasonable time for investigation before publicly disclosing any security issue.
    • Provide Relevant Information – When reporting a security concern, please include as much relevant information as possible, including the nature of the issue, the steps required to reproduce it (where applicable) and any supporting screenshots or evidence.
    • Investigation & Response – We review all reported security concerns and will investigate them as appropriate. Where necessary, we will implement corrective measures and communicate with affected users in accordance with applicable legal obligations.
    • No Unauthorised Testing – We appreciate responsible security research, however users must not intentionally disrupt the platform, access information without authorisation or exploit vulnerabilities while investigating potential security issues.

    Help Keep EdgeBreak Secure

    If you discover a security issue or believe your account has been compromised, please contact us as soon as possible. Responsible reporting helps us protect the EdgeBreak community and continuously improve platform security.
