How The Smart Money Filter Works

The EdgeBreak Smart Money Filter is designed to identify stocks displaying accumulation characteristics commonly associated with increasing institutional participation. Rather than attempting to predict future price movements, the filter searches for evidence of unusual buying activity through price behaviour, volume patterns and market structure.

Institutional Activity

Large investors and institutions often accumulate positions gradually over weeks or months rather than entering a position all at once.

Accumulation Detection

The Smart Money Filter searches for volume clustering, absorption, strong closes and tight price structure that may indicate accumulation activity.

What Does The Smart Money Filter Look For?

The EdgeBreak Smart Money Filter is not based on RSI, MACD or traditional technical indicators. Instead, it focuses on price and volume behaviour that may indicate accumulation by larger market participants.


The filter searches for multiple accumulation characteristics including volume clustering, absorption, strong closes, tight price structure and healthy market trends. Stocks displaying these characteristics can then be filtered directly inside the NASDAQ Scanner alongside breakout-focused criteria such as resistance touches and higher lows.


This approach allows investors to focus on stocks that are not only displaying improving market structure, but may also be attracting increasing market participation before a breakout becomes obvious.
