    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to the questions we receive most often about EdgeBreak and the platform.

    What is EdgeBreak?

    EdgeBreak is a NASDAQ stock research platform providing structured market research tools, educational resources and transparent opportunity tracking. It is designed to help investors research market structure, breakout characteristics and price action.

    Does EdgeBreak provide financial advice?

    No. EdgeBreak provides educational content and structured market research tools. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations or personal financial services.

    How often is the NASDAQ Scanner updated?

    The NASDAQ Scanner operates using an end-of-day process and is updated after each NASDAQ trading session using predefined research criteria.

    What tools are included?

    EdgeBreak includes the NASDAQ Scanner, Smart Money Filter, EdgeBreak Trades, My Workspace, the Trading Guide and a growing educational knowledge base.

    Where should I start?

    Begin with the Trading Guide to understand the EdgeBreak research methodology, then explore the NASDAQ Scanner, Smart Money Filter and educational articles.
