Frequently Asked Questions

Is EdgeBreak a signal service?

No. EdgeBreak does not provide signals or tell you what to buy or sell. We deliver structured breakout setups so you can make your own decisions.

Do you execute trades for me?

No. EdgeBreak is not a broker. You use your own trading platform and stay in full control.

What makes EdgeBreak different?

Instead of scanning charts manually or relying on indicators, EdgeBreak identifies structured breakout setups and ranks them by quality — saving you time and reducing noise.

How often is the watchlist updated?

The watchlist is updated daily after the market closes, delivering fresh breakout opportunities without constant monitoring.

What do the grades mean?

Each setup is scored and graded from C to A+. Higher grades indicate stronger structure and higher-quality breakout conditions.

Why are some setups locked?

Free users can access lower-grade setups. Higher-quality setups (A / B / A+) are part of the Pro plan.

Is this financial advice?

No. EdgeBreak provides data and analysis only. All trading decisions are your responsibility.
