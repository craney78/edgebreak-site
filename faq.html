FAQ

Common questions about EdgeBreak and how the system works

What is EdgeBreak?

EdgeBreak is a structured, data-driven trading system designed to analyse and track breakout setups in the NASDAQ market.

Is this financial advice?

No. EdgeBreak provides data and analysis only. All trading decisions and outcomes are the responsibility of the user.

How often is the system updated?

The system is updated daily after market close based on end-of-day market data and structured analysis.

Why are there sometimes no setups?

The system only highlights setups that meet defined criteria. Fewer setups may occur during certain market conditions.

What market does EdgeBreak focus on?

The system focuses on NASDAQ-listed stocks, where breakout behaviour can be analysed across a broad range of companies.

What do the trade grades mean?

Trade grades reflect the characteristics of each setup based on factors such as price structure, volume behaviour, and breakout conditions.

How do I get access?

You can unlock full access through the pricing page to explore the trading desk, tracked positions, and performance data.
