Frequently Asked Questions

What is EdgeBreak?
EdgeBreak is a breakout intelligence platform that scans thousands of stocks to identify structured breakout setups and track how they perform over time.
How does EdgeBreak work?
EdgeBreak uses a rules-based scanning engine to identify breakout structures, combined with a market conditions model that helps determine when those setups are more likely to perform.
Are these trading signals?
No. EdgeBreak does not provide buy or sell signals. It delivers a curated watchlist of high-quality breakout setups, supported by market conditions, allowing users to evaluate opportunities within a structured framework.
Do I need trading experience to use EdgeBreak?
EdgeBreak is designed to simplify the process of identifying setups, making it useful for both newer and more experienced market participants.
When should I use EdgeBreak?
EdgeBreak is most effective when used alongside market conditions. Understanding whether the market is strong, neutral, or weak is a key part of how setups are evaluated.
What markets does EdgeBreak cover?
EdgeBreak currently focuses on US equities, scanning a large universe of stocks to identify potential breakout structures.
Is EdgeBreak a broker?
No. EdgeBreak does not execute trades. Users trade through their own preferred broker.
How often is the data updated?
The system scans the market daily and updates the watchlist with new breakout setups and market condition data.
Does EdgeBreak guarantee results?
No. All market activity involves risk. EdgeBreak provides structured data and context, but outcomes will always vary.
