Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about EdgeBreak, the NASDAQ Scanner, Smart Money Filter and EdgeBreak Trades

What is EdgeBreak?

EdgeBreak is a NASDAQ stock research platform that helps investors analyse accumulation characteristics, breakout opportunities and market structure using a structured, data-driven framework. Learn more on the About EdgeBreak page.

Does EdgeBreak provide financial advice?

No. EdgeBreak provides educational content, market research and stock screening tools. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations or personal financial services. Please review our Disclaimer for more information.

What is the NASDAQ Scanner?

The NASDAQ Scanner allows investors to search thousands of NASDAQ-listed stocks using filters such as resistance touches, higher lows, Smart Money signals, volume expansion and breakout characteristics. Explore the NASDAQ Scanner.

What is the Smart Money Filter?

The Smart Money Filter identifies stocks displaying accumulation characteristics such as volume clustering, absorption, strong closes and tight price structure. Learn more about the Smart Money Filter and how it integrates with the NASDAQ Scanner.

What stocks does EdgeBreak analyse?

EdgeBreak focuses exclusively on NASDAQ-listed stocks, allowing a consistent research framework to be applied across more than 3,000 companies. Discover more through the Trading Guide.

What are EdgeBreak Trades?

EdgeBreak Trades tracks active and historical positions identified through the EdgeBreak framework. Every trade remains visible to provide a transparent record of outcomes over time. View EdgeBreak Trades.

How do I learn the EdgeBreak framework?

New members can start with the Trading Guide, explore Trading Insights and review the Smart Money Filter explanation to better understand how EdgeBreak approaches market research.

How often is EdgeBreak updated?

EdgeBreak operates using an end-of-day process. Scanner results, watchlists, research tools and trade tracking are updated daily after the market closes.

Ready to explore the NASDAQ market?
