Typical Breakout Structure

One of the most common patterns identified by the EdgeBreak framework.

Before Breakout

Resistance remains unchanged while buyers continue stepping in at progressively higher prices.

After Breakout

Price breaks above resistance with expanding participation and improving market structure.
