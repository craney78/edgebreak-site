How to Use EdgeBreak

A structured, data-driven approach to identifying and managing breakout trades.

Important Disclaimer

EdgeBreak is a trading tool designed to identify potential breakout opportunities using historical data and structured rules.

It does not provide financial advice. All trading involves risk, and losses are a normal part of the process.

You are fully responsible for your own decisions and how you use the information provided.

What EdgeBreak Provides

EdgeBreak removes the need to manually scan hundreds of charts.

It identifies high-probability breakout setups and tracks them through their full lifecycle.

Every trade is recorded, measured, and reflected in the system’s performance.

The Process

1. Identification
The system scans the market for breakout conditions including price structure, resistance levels, and volume confirmation.

2. Execution Awareness
When a valid setup is detected, it appears in the live watchlist.

3. Trade Management
Trades are monitored using predefined rules based on price behaviour and time.

4. Exit
Trades are closed when conditions change, momentum fades, or risk increases.

How You Use It

Each day, the process is simple:

• Review the live watchlist

• Check new trades opened in the activity feed

• Monitor active trades

• Allow the system to guide structure and timing

Expectations & Market Reality

Markets are unpredictable. Not every trade will be profitable.

There will be losing trades, drawdowns, and periods where performance slows.

Strong past performance does not guarantee future results.

The Edge

The strength of EdgeBreak is not in predicting outcomes.

It lies in consistently identifying structured opportunities and applying the same process over time.

Over a series of trades, this consistency is where performance is built.

Key Principles

• Follow a consistent approach

• Manage risk on every trade

• Avoid emotional decision making

• Focus on long-term outcomes, not individual trades

Final Note

EdgeBreak is a tool designed to support disciplined trading.

It provides structure, not certainty.

How you use that structure determines your outcome.
