How to Use EdgeBreak

Simple system. Structured process. Consistent execution.

Important Disclaimer

EdgeBreak is a trading tool designed to identify potential breakout opportunities using historical data and structured rules.

It does not provide financial advice. All trading involves risk, and losses are a normal part of the process.

You are fully responsible for your own decisions and how you use the information provided.

What EdgeBreak Provides

EdgeBreak removes the need to manually scan hundreds of charts each day.

Using a structured end-of-day process, it identifies high-quality breakout setups based on strict filtering across price, volume, and structure.

The system is updated after market close, allowing you to review activity and prepare for the next trading session.

All trades are tracked through their full lifecycle and reflected in overall performance.

Trade Frequency & Selectivity

EdgeBreak is not designed to generate constant trade signals.

The system focuses on identifying only high-quality setups that meet strict criteria.

This means there may be periods with fewer trades, particularly in weaker market conditions.

Rather than forcing activity, the system prioritises selectivity — waiting for the right opportunities instead of chasing every move.

The Process

1. Scan
System identifies structured breakout setups
2. Review
Trades appear in the live watchlist
3. Monitor
Active trades are tracked over time
4. Exit
Trades close based on predefined rules

How You Use It

The system is designed to be reviewed once per day, after the market closes.

At this time, EdgeBreak updates all activity, including newly identified setups and changes to existing trades.

The typical workflow is:

• Check the platform after market close

• Review any new trades added to the system

• Review updates to existing positions

• Prepare for the next trading session based on the system output

EdgeBreak is built around a structured, rules-based approach. It is not designed for emotional or reactive decision making.

Consistency comes from following a repeatable process over time, rather than reacting to individual trades or short-term outcomes.

The platform provides the structure — how that structure is applied remains the responsibility of the user.

Remove emotion. Follow structure. Stay consistent.

Expectations & Market Reality

Markets move in cycles, and not every period will produce strong breakout conditions.

There will be losing trades, drawdowns, and periods where performance slows.

Trade frequency may vary depending on market conditions, as the system prioritises quality over quantity.

EdgeBreak is designed to participate when conditions align — not to force trades when they don’t.

Past performance does not guarantee future results.

The Edge

The strength of EdgeBreak is not in predicting outcomes.

It lies in consistently identifying structured opportunities and applying the same process over time.

Over a series of trades, this consistency is where performance is built.

Key Principles

• Follow a consistent approach

• Manage risk on every trade

• Avoid emotional decision making

• Focus on long-term outcomes, not individual trades

Final Note

EdgeBreak is a tool designed to support disciplined trading.

It provides structure, not certainty.

How you use that structure determines your outcome.
