FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

EdgeBreak Academy FAQs

Answers to some of the most common questions about EdgeBreak Academy.

Is EdgeBreak Academy free?

Yes. Every lesson within EdgeBreak Academy is available free of charge. Simply create a free EdgeBreak account to save your learning progress.

Do I need trading experience?

No. The Academy begins with the fundamentals before progressing to more advanced market research concepts, making it suitable for beginners and experienced investors alike.

Does EdgeBreak provide financial advice?

No. EdgeBreak Academy is an educational resource designed to teach structured market research. It does not provide financial advice or investment recommendations.

What will I learn?

You'll learn market structure, support and resistance, accumulation, volume analysis, breakout characteristics and how to use the EdgeBreak research platform.

What is EdgeBreak Pro?

EdgeBreak Pro provides access to advanced research tools including the NASDAQ Scanner, Breakout Scanner, Smart Money Filter and My Workspace to help organise and analyse market opportunities.

Can I complete the Academy at my own pace?

Yes. There are no time limits or deadlines. Complete the lessons whenever it suits you and revisit them whenever you need a refresher.

IMPORTANT

Educational Content Only

EdgeBreak Academy has been developed to teach structured market research using historical market data, price action and educational examples.

The Academy does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations or trading signals. All examples are presented for educational purposes only and should not be interpreted as a recommendation to buy or sell any financial instrument.

Always conduct your own independent research and consider seeking advice from a licensed financial professional before making investment decisions.
