    Danger Zone

    The actions below are permanent and may result in the loss of access to your EdgeBreak account and associated data. Please proceed carefully.

    Permanently delete your EdgeBreak account, profile, workspace, journal entries, notes, watchlists, alerts and connected broker information. This action cannot be undone.

    Remove all connected brokerage accounts from EdgeBreak. This will stop portfolio synchronisation, order management and broker connectivity until a new connection is established.

    Cancel your EdgeBreak Membership. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period unless otherwise stated by your subscription provider.

    If you're experiencing issues with your account or are considering deleting it, our support team may be able to help before permanent action is taken.
