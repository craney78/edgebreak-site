How EdgeBreak Works

EdgeBreak tracks breakout setups across the NASDAQ using defined rules based on price structure and volume. Trades are recorded from entry to exit, with notifications sent when positions are opened and closed so you can follow the system’s activity. Each trade includes a chart showing entry and risk levels, while positions are typically allowed to run as trends develop. A personal portfolio tracker is included so you can monitor your own performance over time. This is not financial advice or a signal service — it is a structured framework for analysing breakout trends.
