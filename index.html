Why EdgeBreak Works

Most traders spend significant time scanning charts, chasing breakouts, and reacting in the wrong conditions.

EdgeBreak removes this inefficiency by systemising the entire process.

The system scans thousands of stocks daily and identifies structured breakout setups — ranked by quality, so you can focus on what actually matters.

In addition, EdgeBreak is powered by the EdgeBreak Market Intelligence Engine — an intelligence-driven approach to real-time market conditions, measured through how breakout setups are actually performing across the market.

This provides critical context — highlighting that conditions are not simply “good” or “bad,” and that strong opportunities can still emerge in weaker environments.

At the same time, even high-quality setups can underperform when conditions are unfavourable — something EdgeBreak is designed to identify and account for.

Breakout setups are not treated equally.

Each setup is evaluated using a structured ranking framework — assessing the quality of the breakout based on key characteristics such as strength, structure, and follow-through behaviour.

This allows opportunities to be graded from C through to A+, helping prioritise higher-quality setups while filtering out weaker formations.

The result is a consistent, objective view of opportunity — rather than relying on subjective judgement or isolated signals.

The EdgeBreak Market Intelligence Engine continuously monitors how breakout setups are performing across the market — providing clarity on when conditions support participation, and when they require greater selectivity.
