    What is EdgeBreak?

    EdgeBreak is a NASDAQ stock research platform designed to help investors identify stocks showing accumulation, buying pressure and breakout potential through simplified stock scanning and research tools.

    What is the Smart Money Filter?

    The Smart Money Filter The Smart Money Filter identifies stocks displaying accumulation characteristics such as volume clustering, absorption, strong closes and tight price structure. The filter can be applied directly inside the NASDAQ Scanner.

    What is the NASDAQ Screener?

    The NASDAQ Screener allows investors to search thousands of NASDAQ stocks using filters such as resistance touches, higher lows, volume expansion and breakout activity.

    What is EdgeBreak Trades?

    EdgeBreak Trades tracks every historical and active trade identified through the EdgeBreak framework. Winners, losers and open positions remain visible to provide complete transparency.

    Can beginners use EdgeBreak?

    Yes. EdgeBreak includes educational resources, trading guides and strategy content designed to help investors improve their understanding of price action, risk management and stock research.

    Does EdgeBreak provide trading education?

    Visit the Trading Guide, Breakout Guide, Strategy and Trading Insights sections to learn more about the framework.

    Is EdgeBreak financial advice?

    No. EdgeBreak is a stock research and educational platform. All information is provided for educational and research purposes only and should not be considered financial advice or investment recommendations.
