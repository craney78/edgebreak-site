Why EdgeBreak Exists

Financial markets generate an enormous amount of information every day. Thousands of stocks, endless news, countless indicators and constant market commentary can make it difficult for investors to focus on what matters most.

EdgeBreak was created to simplify that process.

Rather than requiring users to manually review hundreds of charts each day, EdgeBreak applies a structured research framework across the NASDAQ market to identify stocks displaying specific characteristics such as accumulation, buying pressure, volume expansion and breakout potential.

The platform was built on a simple belief: better decisions start with better research.

By focusing exclusively on NASDAQ-listed stocks, EdgeBreak is able to provide a more targeted and consistent research experience rather than attempting to cover every market and asset class.

Transparency is a core part of the platform. Opportunities identified by the framework are tracked over time, allowing users to review both successful and unsuccessful outcomes through a single historical record.

EdgeBreak is not designed to predict markets or provide investment recommendations. Its purpose is to help investors organise information, identify opportunities for further research and better understand how structured market analysis performs over time.

The goal is simple:

To make structured NASDAQ stock research more accessible through data, transparency and consistency.

How the Platform Works

EdgeBreak operates using a structured end-of-day process. Market data is analysed after each trading session and opportunities are identified using predefined price action, volume and market structure criteria.

Opportunities are then tracked over time, allowing users to review how setups develop and compare current market activity with historical examples.

The platform is designed to be reviewed once per day, helping investors maintain a consistent research process without the pressure of monitoring markets throughout the day.

Our Approach

EdgeBreak is built on the belief that consistency comes from following a structured research process rather than attempting to predict individual market outcomes.

By applying the same methodology across the NASDAQ market each day, the platform aims to reduce noise and help users focus on stocks displaying specific research characteristics.

Transparency remains central to the framework, with tracked opportunities recorded over time to provide a balanced view of historical performance.

Where We're Based
