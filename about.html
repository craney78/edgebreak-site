About EdgeBreak

A structured, data-driven platform for analysing and tracking breakout behaviour in the NASDAQ market

What We Do

EdgeBreak is a software platform that analyses and tracks breakout setups using structured market data, historical patterns, and consistent rules.

The system focuses on analysing and monitoring breakout conditions across a wide range of NASDAQ-listed stocks, presenting this information in a clear and structured format.

Rather than overwhelming users with constant activity, EdgeBreak is designed around selectivity — highlighting setups that meet defined criteria and tracking them over time.

What You Get From EdgeBreak

EdgeBreak is built to support a more disciplined and data-driven approach.

  • Track breakout setups and how they develop over time
  • Monitor performance across multiple trades and market conditions
  • Understand patterns in win rate, returns, and consistency
  • Reduce noise by focusing on structured setups rather than constant signals
  • Build a repeatable process based on data rather than emotion

The platform provides the structure — how that structure is applied remains the responsibility of the user.

How the Platform Works

EdgeBreak operates using a structured end-of-day process.

Market data is analysed after each trading session, and breakout setups are highlighted based on predefined criteria including price behaviour, volume patterns, and structural conditions.

These setups are then tracked over time, allowing users to observe how they evolve and how performance develops across multiple trades.

The platform is designed to be reviewed once per day, helping users stay consistent without the pressure of real-time decision making.

Our Approach

EdgeBreak is built on the idea that consistency comes from following a structured process over time, not from predicting individual outcomes.

Markets move in cycles, and not every period will produce strong conditions. The system is designed to reflect that reality by prioritising selectivity and consistency over constant activity.

By tracking both positive and negative outcomes, EdgeBreak provides a balanced view of performance and reinforces a long-term, disciplined approach.

Where We’re Based

EdgeBreak is developed and operated from Australia, with a focus on delivering a globally accessible, high-performance trading platform.
